Gourmetbillys

Ingredients

  • 2 billys pan pizzas
  • Some kebab perhaps or some other topping.

Directions

  1. Start by preparing the 2 billys in the microwave for a minute or so.
  2. Stack the billys in a pan facing eachother, before hand you should add your topping of choice inbetween the billys.
  3. Fry in a pan until it gets a nice and crispy surface.
  4. Enjoy your Gourmetbillys!
