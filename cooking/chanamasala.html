Ingredients

  • 1 onion
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 2 tpbs curry powder
  • 1 tps cumin
  • 1/2 tps turmeric
  • 1/2 tps paprika
  • 1 tps salt
  • 1 Can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 Can coconut milk
  • 1 Can chickpeas
  • 1 handful spinach

Directions

  1. Fry the diced onion and garlic in olive oil, then add the spices.
  2. Add the tomatoes, coconut milk and chickpeas and cook for ~8min.
  3. Stir in the spinach until it is wilted.
