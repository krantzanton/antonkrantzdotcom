Ingredients

  • 1 packet of bacon
  • 1 onion
  • 500g mince meat
  • 2 dl brown sugar
  • 2 dl ketchup
  • Some BBQ flavor or sauce
  • approx 6 packets of beans, whatever you like. I had 3 white, 2 kidney and 1 black
  • salt and pepper

Directions

  1. Fry the bacon in a pot then add onion.
  2. Add the mince meat, brown sugar and ketchup and cook.
  3. Add the BBQ sauce and the beans and salt and pepper then cook for 45 minutes.
