Ragu

Served with pasta or rice, approx 125g pasta per portion

Ingredients

  • 500 g mince meat
  • 1-2 carrots
  • 1 big onion
  • 2 cellery stalks
  • 2-4 garlic cloves
  • Thyme
  • 1 Can passed tomatoes
  • 1 beef buillion

Seasoned with knorr aromat or just regular salt

Directions

  1. Mix sugar and flour in a bowl
  2. Add water and mix
  3. Bake in oven for 30 minutes
